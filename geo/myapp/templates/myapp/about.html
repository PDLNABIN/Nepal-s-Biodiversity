About the Application

Nepal’s Biodiversity Chronicles is an interactive platform that enables users to explore the diverse ecosystem of Nepal. Through real-time maps, species distribution data, and geospatial insights, the application aims to promote biodiversity awareness and conservation efforts. Whether you're a researcher, conservationist, or simply an enthusiast, this application provides valuable information to help you understand Nepal's natural richness.
